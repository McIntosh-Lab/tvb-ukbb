The 'TVB_SchaeferHarvOx_414' parcellation was created by combining the Schaefer2018 400 ROI cortical parcellation with the Harvard-Oxford subcortical parcellation distributed with FSL.
Detailed label information can be found in the associated lookup tables (*_LUT.tsv).

Citations:
Schaefer A, Kong R, Gordon EM, Laumann TO, Zuo XN, Holmes AJ, Eickhoff SB, Yeo BTT (2018) Local-Global parcellation of the human cerebral cortex from intrinsic functional connectivity MRI. Cerebral Cortex, 29:3095-3114. doi: 10.1093/cercor/bhx179
