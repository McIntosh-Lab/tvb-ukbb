The Regional-Map 96 (RM96) parcellation contains 82 cortical ROIs and 14 subcortical ROIs. It is described in Bezgin et al (2017).

Citation:
Bezgin G, Solodkin A, Bakker R, Ritter P, McIntosh AR (2017) Mapping complementary features of cross-species structural connectivity to construct realistic "Virtual Brains". Human Brain Mapping, 38: 2080-2093. doi: 10.1002/hbm.23506
